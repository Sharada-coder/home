GetWarmr

Founder

Founded in 2021

GetWarmr is a humanitarian initiative that provides handmade crocheted scarves to local shelters supporting unhoused individuals in Jersey City and New York City. We also contribute crocheted baby hats to memory boxes offered by organizations that serve bereaved parents, offering comfort during times of profound loss.
